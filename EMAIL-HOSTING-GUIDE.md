## üöÄ How to Set Up Email (After Domain Registration)

### **Option A: Zoho Mail (Recommended - FREE)**

1. **Sign up for Zoho Mail:**
   - Go to: https://www.zoho.com/mail/
   - Click "Sign Up Free"
   - Choose "Business Email" plan (FREE)

2. **Add Your Domain:**
   - Enter: `alsahrapml.com`
   - Verify domain ownership (add DNS records)

3. **Add DNS Records in Spaceship:**
   - Go to Spaceship DNS management
   - Add MX records (provided by Zoho)
   - Add TXT records (for verification)

4. **Create Email Addresses:**
   - Create: `export@alsahrapml.com`
   - Create: `info@alsahrapml.com`
   - Create: `contact@alsahrapml.com`

5. **Access Your Email:**
   - Webmail: https://mail.zoho.com
   - Mobile apps available

**Full Guide:** Zoho provides step-by-step instructions during setup

---

### **Option B: Google Workspace**

1. **Sign up for Google Workspace:**
   - Go to: https://workspace.google.com/
   - Click "Get Started"
   - Choose "Business Starter" ($6/user/month)

2. **Add Your Domain:**
   - Enter: `alsahrapml.com`
   - Verify domain ownership

3. **Add DNS Records:**
   - Add MX records in Spaceship
   - Add TXT records for verification

4. **Create Users:**
   - Create: `export@alsahrapml.com`
   - Create other email addresses as needed

5. **Access:**
   - Gmail interface: https://mail.google.com
   - Mobile apps available
