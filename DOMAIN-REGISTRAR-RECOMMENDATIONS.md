## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Tips

1. **Check Renewal Prices:**
   - Some registrars offer cheap first-year prices but high renewals
   - Look at long-term costs (3-5 years)

2. **Free Privacy Protection:**
   - Most registrars now include this for free
   - Protects your personal information from public WHOIS database

3. **DNS Management:**
   - Make sure your registrar allows DNS management
   - You'll need to add A records and CNAME records

4. **Transfer Policy:**
   - Check if you can transfer the domain later if needed
   - Some registrars lock domains for 60 days after registration

5. **Email Forwarding:**
   - Some registrars offer free email forwarding
   - Useful for `export@alsahrapml.com` email addresses
